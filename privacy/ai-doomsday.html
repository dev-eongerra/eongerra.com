Privacy Policy

Short version: AI Doomsday collects nothing. It runs entirely on your device.

English

AI Doomsday does not collect, transmit, or upload any personal information.

All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device and are never sent to any server.

The game runs fully offline and integrates no analytics, tracking, ads, or third-party services. Bundled media, such as background music, is included with the app itself.

Uninstalling the app removes all of its related data completely.

For any privacy questions, contact us at dev@eongerra.com.
